# Emma Wichmann | Career Profile

## Profile

I began exploring coding in 2022 while studying for the MCAT and customizing my `Anki` cards. A lot of medical-school Anki workflows rely on card templates and formatting features such as `Basic`, `Basic + Reverse`, `Cloze`, and `Image Occlusion`, and Anki cards can also use `HTML`, `CSS`, and some `JavaScript` in their templates and styling. Working with those systems was one of my earliest entry points into code.

Later, during medical school, I also used AI tools to help create study materials and practice exams. Over time, that interest grew into wanting to understand the code itself, not just use tools built by other people.

I am building a portfolio of personal, front-end, and accessibility-focused projects while strengthening my foundations in `HTML`, `CSS`, `JavaScript`, and eventually `Python`.
